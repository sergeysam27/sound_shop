Welcome To Uniqlo Store

Avante Audio is a professional audio brand born from a passion for excellence in sound reproduction. Avante believes in producing high-end products – that offer both sonic and aesthetic beauty – at attainable prices in order to provide the greatest possible audio experience for its users. A division of the ADJ Group of Companies, Avante Audio is an independent business unit focused on developing, manufacturing and marketing innovative professional audio equipment. Created for musicians and bands, houses of worship, audio production companies, rental houses, and system integrators, Avante Audio’s Achromic Series is a complete range of active loudspeaker cabinets that offer powerful output, detailed audio clarity, built-in DSP and sleek exterior designs. Meanwhile, the new Imperio mini line array system demonstrates Avante Audio’s commitment to audio innovation, offering a flexible modular design that can be easily deployed in a variety of configurations including flown and ground-stacked setups. This makes the system ideal both for installation in medium-sized venues that aren’t large enough to warrant a traditional full-size array system as well as for use by production companies looking for a flexible active speaker system that can be deployed in various arrangements to suit a wide variety of different applications. The Avante range also offers compact and portable audio products, such as the new AS8 column system, that are ideal for gigging duos and solo musicians as well as mobile entertainers.
